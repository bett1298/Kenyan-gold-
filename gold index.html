About Kenyan Gold

Kenyan Gold is Citizen TV's flagship agricultural program dedicated to showcasing the incredible stories of resilience, innovation, and success from Kenya's farming communities.

Through expert insights, community interactions, and market connections, we bring the untold stories of our agricultural heroes to your screens every week.

Our mission is to inspire, educate, and empower farmers and agribusiness entrepreneurs across Kenya, connecting them with opportunities that transform lives and communities.
