Our Mission

The Kenyan Gold Program is dedicated to discovering, showcasing, and celebrating local gold mining and jewelry ventures across Kenya. Our aim is to empower local communities, promote transparency in the gold trade, and highlight innovative entrepreneurs in the sector.

What We Do

Through investigative stories, business profiles, and community initiatives, we provide insights into the gold industry in Kenya. We also educate citizens on safe investment and ethical mining practices.
